Poseidon Aerospace

Intern
Avionics & Composite Manufacturing

Poseidon Aerospace is a company that specializes in the manufacturing of autonomous unmanned cargo aircraft that are aimed at moving cargo faster and cheaper compared to traditional air logistics. The company has developed two unmanned cargo aircraft, Egret and Heron, which are manufactured using carbon fiber materials as well as other composite materials. The company has also received $11M in seed funding and operates from California, Washington, and Maine.

As an intern at Poseidon Aerospace, I had a hands-on experience within the avionics as well as composite manufacturing areas of the company.

Assisted in Avionics Integration & Wiring for Autonomous Flight Control Systems for Unmanned Aircraft Platforms
Conducted Composite Layup & Fabrication for Airframe Parts composed of Carbon Fiber Materials
Worked in a fast-paced startup environment with engineers from Amazon, L3Harris, & Lockheed Martin
Got exposure to the relationship between software systems and physical hardware.

Poker Bot

01

A computer program that plays poker with other players with a positive expected value. The bot assesses its hand strength, its opponents' behavior, and decides on its betting strategy through probability theory and heuristics from game strategy.

Python Probability Theory Decision Logic Game Strategy
Problem Playing Poker involves making decisions under conditions of uncertainty. Poker involves evaluating incomplete information, its opponents' behavior, and deciding on actions that maximize its expected profit.
What I Built A decision engine that determines equity, odds, and expected value for every decision. The bot adapts to its position, betting sizes, and its opponents' behavior.
